Maximilian Speicher
I'm a computer scientist who fell in with design. My work as a postdoctoral research fellow at the University of Michigan School of Information is strongly focused on crowdsourcing & natural user interfaces. Prior to that, for two years, I've helped grow HoloBuilder Inc., an AR/VR start-up, as their VP of Data Analytics. In 2016, I received my Ph.D. degree from Chemnitz University of Technology. Before, I obtained a M.Sc. in Computer Science from ETH Zurich & a B.Sc. in the same field from RWTH Aachen University, minoring in Business Administration.

Also, I'm a Ringtennis enthusiast & the men's captain of the German national Ringtennis team.
